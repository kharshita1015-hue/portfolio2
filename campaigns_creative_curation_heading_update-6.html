About Me

Sociology grad. Content designer. Slightly obsessed with scrapbooking.

I enjoy turning ideas into content that's thoughtful, clear, and visually engaging. Whether it's a social media post, a campaign, or an editorial layout, I like finding the simplest way to tell the story.

Design gradually became the part of the process I enjoyed the most. Sociology gave me a foundation in understanding people and communication, while content design became the space where I found myself bringing those ideas to life visually.

Most days you'll find me somewhere between Canva, Pinterest boards, travel inspiration, and a scrapbook that probably didn't need one more page… but got one anyway.
